Welcome to Standard Club,

We know how important golf is to you, here at Standard we believe in more time playing and less time waiting. Standard Club has provided its members with excellent service and outstanding amenities for over a century. Join us and experience our beautiful Zoysia fairways for spectacular golf play, Har-Tru Clay Tennis Courts and Pool. We are proud to announce that Standard Club has a newly restructured membership plan to allow for more choices and flexibility for our members. Standard Club is creating a new and innovative club experience that you will not find anywhere in the Louisville area! Come out for a tour today and we hope to see you soon! Standard Club is conveniently located off of Brownsboro Road in eastern Jefferson County.
